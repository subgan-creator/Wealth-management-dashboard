## Presentation Tips

**Timing:**
- Main presentation: 15-20 minutes
- Q&A: 10-15 minutes
- Demo (optional): 20-30 minutes

**Engagement Tactics:**
- Start with a real client scenario
- Use specific examples from their business
- Pause for questions after each major section
- Show live demo if possible
- End with clear next steps

**Key Talking Points:**
- Listen for pain points and address them specifically
- Emphasize "no IT required" for configuration
- Highlight time savings and ROI
- Address compliance and security concerns proactively
- Offer personalized demo with their business events
